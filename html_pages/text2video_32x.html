Text-to-Video Generation (REGEN-32× Latent Space)

We provide visual examples for text-to-video generation on our ultra-compact latent space with 32x temporal compression and the videos are generated at the resolution of 192x320. The early stage results suggest that we could generate videos with plausible content with 5x reduction in the number of latent frames compared to current state-of-the-art MAGVIT-v2.
Note: Our goal is not to compete with the state-of–the-art text-to-video generation method, but just to show that our ultra-compact latent space is compatible for text-to-video generation.
